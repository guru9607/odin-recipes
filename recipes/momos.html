Momos

Be it a simple sandwitch or an elaborate burger, the joy you get from a filling, hearty and delicious sandwich is amazing.

Don't make boring sandwich when you have our delicious recipe to make it mouth watery.

Steps to make delicious Momos are:

  1. Firstly, prepare stuffing by heating 3 tsp oil and saute 3 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger and 2 chilli.
  2. Also, add 2 tbsp spring onion and saute on high flame.
  3. Further, add 1 cup carrot and 2 cup cabbage, stir fry on high flame and add ½ tsp pepper and ½ tsp salt.
  4. Stuffing mixture is ready, further, pinch a small ball sized momos dough and flatten.
  5. Now dust with some maida and start to roll using a rolling pin, roll to almost medium thin circle, around 4 – 5 inch in diameter. Make sure you roll from sides and keep the centre slightly thick.
  6. Now place a heaped tbsp of prepared stuffing in the centre.Start pleating the edges slowly and gather everything.Press in middle and seal the momos forming a bundle.
  7. Heat a steamer and arrange the momos in the tray without touching each other.
  8. Furthermore, steam momos for 10-12 minutes or till shiny sheen appears over it.
  9. Finally, veg momos recipe is ready to enjoy with momos chutney.
