Sandwich

Be it a simple sandwitch or an elaborate burger, the joy you get from a filling, hearty and delicious sandwich is amazing.

Don't make boring sandwich when you have our delicious recipe to make it mouth watery.

Steps to make Delicious Sandwich are:

  1. Firstly, place a slice of bread on the plate.
  2. Second, put the tomato sauce.
  3. Then, add salad, slice tomatoes, cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, tomato sauce on it.
  4. Next, add lettuce leave.
  5. After that, put a slice of bread on the top to cover them.
  6. Finally, serve it!
